About Us

Our Mission

Our company is an eco-friendly company. We use alternative/healthy material (tencel fibers/pulp from eucalyptus trees) to make plaid apparel for both women and men. We are also making sure our customers further inform themselves on how to conserve energy and water in their daily lives. We are attracting a larger audience by having customized college plaid shirts to make more profit for our business and to plant more trees.

How To Help The Envrionment

Water

Turn off tap while brushing teeth/scrubbing with body soap/shampooing/washing hands... etc

Fix leaks around your home

Water your plants in the morning, it is cooler and you’ll use less water

Use clothes washer only for full loads of clothes

Use a broom, not a hose to clean driveways

Flush the toilet when you have peed twice, you do not need to flush everytime.

Energy

Unplug electronics and appliances when not in use

Running your air conditioning at 78°F instead of 72°F can save between 6% - 18% percent on your cooling bill

Lighting makes up about 10 percent of home energy costs. Save up to 75 percent of that energy by replacing incandescent bulbs with compact fluorescent bulbs (CFLs). They also last longer, saving money on replacements.
