Better Than New

The single best thing we can do for our planet is keep clothing material in use longer and cut down on consumption. Upcycling helps reduce carbon dioxide emissions by using old materials instead of new ones. In fact, for every ton of discarded textiles used again, twenty tons of carbon dioxide is prevented from entering the atmosphere. It also promotes sustainable innovation and creativity, and helps preserve our precious resources.

Trade It In

Trade in any of your used or old clothing in exchange for Euca store credit. Donating clothing to us does not only benefit yourself, but also benfits the environment by reducing textile waste.

Upcycle It

We will upcycle your clothing high-quality apparel that can last for years. Tranforming your clothing reduces the need for raw materials which means a reduction in air pollution, water pollution, greenhouse gas emissions.
