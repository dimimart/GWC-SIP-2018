COLLEGE PARTNERSHIP

Many colleges and universities in California have sponsored our company to design personalized flannels matching their school colors. Our goal by 2020 is to have flannels for universities all over the United States.

OUR COMMUNITY

For every purchase, a eucalyptus tree is planted in Austrailia and we give back to the sustainable farmers who have partnered with us to create our apparel. Our social business with the farmers is a fair trading as we share our profits for their tencel fibers.

ECO-FRIENDLY

Sustainability is the core of our business. All of our products are constructed by tencel fibers and recycled clothing as an alternative material for cotton. Our tencel fibers are extracted pulp of eucalytptus trees in Austrailia and does not require any harm to our trees.
